It's time to begin again

August 16, 2021

Perhaps that should be our motto this year. With the pandemic possibly winding down and in-person classes set to resume, it seems it’s finally time to start working our way back to normal. In my case, that also means rebuilding my website, having recently discovered it transformed into a smoking heap of rubble…

Sometime over the course of the pandemic there was a massive data breach at WordPress. This news didn’t reach me at the time, probably because I wasn’t thinking about my website at all. Not only was I buried knee-deep in Canvas running virtual courses, my second child was also days away from being born. So checking my Wordpress site never once crossed my mind.

Fast forward to a week ago, when I start thinking about Fall quarter and decide to check out my old site. Yikes. I spent a few hours poking around the ashes to see if I could easily fix the damage, but eventually I came to the conclusion that it wasn’t worth the effort. The site had been feeling a bit stale and needed an overhaul anyway, so this seemed like as good a time as any to start over fresh. To quote Normon Juster:

"Sometimes I find the best way of getting from one place to another is simply to erase everything and begin again.

Norton Juster, The Phantom Tollboth

I spent the last week learning abot Git, GitHub, GitHub Pages, and Jekyll. I’ve also tracked down this lovely Tufte-style theme. Now all that remains is the real work: putting up content. I plan to make this a home for everything I’m working on, from serious research to fun diversions. So let’s get to it!

It's time to begin again - August 16, 2021 - Robert Easton
